Return & refund flow

Returns are customer-shipped after approval. Refund-only cases are processed directly by the merchant. Every step is visible inside your order detail page.

1
Request after-sales

2

3

Return shipments are confirmed by merchants.
4
After-sales closed
Order status updates when the case ends.

FAQ

Quick answers to top questions.

Add items to your cart from multiple stores. NovaMart will split orders by merchant but still allow a single checkout and payment flow.

When a return/refund is requested, the order status switches to After-sales until the request is resolved and closed.

Points are earned only on the final paid amount. If a refund is issued, the related points are adjusted automatically.
