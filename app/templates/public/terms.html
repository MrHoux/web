Account & access

Create one account per person. Keep your login secure and update your profile details as needed. We may suspend accounts that violate policy or security requirements.

Orders & payments

Orders are split by merchant but paid in a single checkout flow. Payments are processed securely. Prices, taxes, and promotions are shown before checkout confirmation.

Returns & after-sales

After-sales requests move an order into a dedicated status. Returns are shipped by customers after approval. Refund-only requests are handled directly by merchants.

User conduct

Respect merchants and support staff. Do not post illegal content, spam, or abusive language. Repeated violations may lead to account restrictions.

Policy changes

We may update these terms as the platform evolves. Significant changes will be communicated in-app.
